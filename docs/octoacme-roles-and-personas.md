# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Design and validate user experiences, working closely with Product and Engineering to ensure solutions are usable and meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Develop wireframes, prototypes, and user flow diagrams
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and synthesize findings
- Create design specifications and accessible UI patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on requirements and Developers on implementation
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences that meet user needs and business goals
- Reduce rework by validating assumptions early
- Ensure design intent is preserved through implementation

### Typical interactions
- Product Manager: refine requirements and success metrics based on user insight
- Developer: hand off designs, clarify edge cases, and support implementation
- QA: validate UX acceptance criteria in builds
- Data Analyst: define instrumentation to measure UX impact

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Collect, analyze, and report data to inform product decisions and measure project outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and instrument events with Product and Engineering
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports for project stakeholders
- Analyze experiment and feature usage data to surface insights
- Run and interpret A/B tests and experiments where applicable
- Partner with Product Managers to translate insights into prioritized actions

### Goals
- Provide reliable, timely data to inform decisions
- Close the loop between outcomes and investments
- Reduce decision uncertainty with measurable signals

### Typical interactions
- Product Manager: agree on metrics, interpretation, and reporting cadence
- Developer: ensure data instrumentation and event schema match requirements
- Project Manager: align outcome reporting with project milestones
- UX Designer: measure impact of design changes on behavior and satisfaction

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinate deployments, manage release calendars, and drive release-related communications between teams to ensure predictable, low-risk releases.

### Responsibilities
- Own and maintain the release plan and calendar
- Coordinate pre-release readiness checks and go/no-go meetings
- Ensure pre-release checklists and rollback plans are complete
- Communicate release scope, timelines, and post-release verification steps to stakeholders
- Facilitate coordination across engineering, QA, support, and product

### Goals
- Improve release predictability and reduce incidents caused by releases
- Ensure clear ownership of deployment steps and communications
- Ensure timely stakeholder notifications and support readiness

### Typical interactions
- Developer & QA: confirm deployment readiness and coordinate rollbacks if needed
- Project Manager: align release timelines with project milestones
- Product Manager: confirm release contents and business priorities
- Support/Operations: coordinate monitoring and on-call expectations
